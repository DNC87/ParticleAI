Taxa included: Ligustrum lucidum, Ligustrum ovalifolium.
Description: evergreen trees, panicle flowers, drupe fruit.
Pollination: entomophilic and anemophilic.
Phenology: spring flowering and early summer, from March to June.
Ecology: cultivated as ornamental.
Pollen morphology: trizonocolporated. Opening diameter 1- (2) μm.

Allergenicity: smaller than the olive tree (high).
