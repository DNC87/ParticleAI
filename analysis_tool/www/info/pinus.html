Taxa included: stone pine (Pinus pinea), pine resin (Pinus pinaster), Aleppo pine (Pinus halepensis), cedar of the Himalayas (Cedrus deodara), Atlas cedar (Cedrus atlantica).
Description: monoecious, evergreen trees; unisexual strobiles.
Pollination: anemophilous.
Phenology: winter/spring flowering, from Frebruary to April.
Ecology: cultivated as forest or ornamental trees, subspecies in holm oaks and cork oaks.
Pollen morphology: unperturbed grains, with two air sacs.

Allergenicity: Low.
