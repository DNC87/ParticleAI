Taxa included: shade banana (Platanus hispanica), oriental shadow banana (Platanus orientalis).
Description: deciduous, monoecious trees; unisexual flowers gathered in glossed unisexual inflorescences.
Pollination: anemophilous.
Phenology: spring flowering, from March to May.
Ecology: cultivated as ornamental.
Pollen morphology: Triconocollar, isopolar, radiosymmetric pollen.

Allergenicity: Moderate.
