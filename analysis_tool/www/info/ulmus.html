Taxa included: Ulmus minor.
Description: deciduous trees, hermaphrodite flowers in glomeruli, samara fruit.
Pollination: anemophilous.
Phenology: winter flowering, from January to February.
Ecology: spontaneous on the edges of water courses and cultivated as ornamental.
Pollen morphology: spheroidal pollen about 23-30 microns in diameter.

Allergenicity: High.
