Taxa included: Casuarina cunninghamiana.
Description: evergreen dioecious trees with squamate leaves on filiform green stems, capsular fruit.
Pollination: anemophilous.
Phenology:winter, from January to February.
Ecology: cultivated as ornamental.
Pollen morphology: trizonoporated grains, 23-30 microns in diameter.

Allergenicity: High.
