Taxa included: Quercus rotundifolia, Quercus suber, Quercus coccifera, Quercus faginea, Quercus pyrenaica.
Description: evergreen trees, marcescent or deciduous; unisexual flowers in catkins; simple and sepaloid perianth.
Pollination: anemophilous.
Phenology: spring flowering, from March to May.
Ecology: forests in general or bushes, holm oaks, cork oaks, coscojares, quegigares and melojares.
Pollen morphology: trizocolporated, psilated to rounded grains.

Allergenicity: High.
