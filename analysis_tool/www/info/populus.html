Taxa included: Populus alba, Populus nigra.
Description: deciduous trees, flowers in catkins, capsular fruit.
Pollination: anemophilous.
Phenology: winter/spring flowering, from Frebruary to March.
Ecology: cultivated next to water courses and as ornamental.
Pollen morphology: unperturbed grains, small to medium size; D = 22-30 μm.

Allergenicity: Moderate.
